WELCOME TO TATYASAHEB KORE INSTITUDE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY WARANA , KOLHAPUR

Tatyasaheb Kore Institute of Engineering and Technology (TKIET), established in 1983, is an Autonomous college, affiliated to Shivaji University, Kolhapur and approved by AICTE. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in the discipline of Engineering & Technology across various specializations.Candidates can seek admission to the bachelor course if they have a non-zero score in MHT-CET 2021 or a non-zero positive score in JEE Main. Tatyasaheb Kore Institute of Engineering and Technology has tie-ups with several leading corporates of India to provide placement assistance to the students. The highest package offered at college in 2021 was INR 24 LPA.

Tatyasaheb Kore Institute of Engineering and Technology Key Facts:
1)TKIET has received accreditation from NBA as well as NAAC.
2)The College was five times consecutively included in the Times Platinum category institute rating by the AICTE-CII survey.
3)Admissions- Candidates who appeared for the GATE exam are eligible to apply for the M.Tech course at TKIET.
4)Placement- 140+ Companies visited TKIET for the campus placement.
5)Application- Interested candidates can apply online for admission to the courses offered by the Tatya Kore Institute.
